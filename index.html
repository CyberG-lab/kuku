Our Story

Proudly Local.
Unapologetically Fresh.

Shaz Poultry was born in Kisumu with a simple belief: Kenyan families deserve access to premium-quality chicken at fair prices. We work directly with trusted local farms, butcher everything fresh every morning, and ensure every cut reaches you at its best — no middlemen, no shortcuts.
